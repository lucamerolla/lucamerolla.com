Luca Merolla

Lives in St-Prex, Switzerland

ICO Advisor, Business Angel, Speaker, Startup Mentor, Blockchain Consultant

Luca is very passionate about Innovation, Crypto Currencies and removing Learning inhibitors from organizations. Luca has a multi disciplinary background in the fields of information technology, business and management and holds a B.Sc. in IT Engineering and an MBA with specialization in international business and venture capital.

During his career, Luca has had the opportunity and the privilege to work in different countries (Ireland, Netherlands, Italy and Switzerland) with fast growing start-ups, large enterprises and as well as consultant for a business incubator.

Luca worked for Swissquote Bank where he was Vice-Director and Member of Senior Management. Luca has helped Swissquote in growing the offshore department up to approximately 150 people. Luca was in charge of the innovation committee which has organized 3 innovation contests (Battle Dev) with more than 100 employees involved in creating innovative ideas in a time-boxed event of 24 hours.

Luca is an ICO Advisor, Business Angel and startup mentor with strong interests in FinTech, Blockchain, Machine Learning and AI topics. Luca is currently CEO of Guruvest an Open Investment Platform which is preparing its Initial Coin Offering on Ethereum blockchain.

Luca is an experienced public speaker and has very strong entrepreneurial characteristics and loves to inspire people to do the things that inspire them.
